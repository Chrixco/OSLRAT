Comprehensive Assessment Tools

OSLRAT provides a suite of powerful, accessible tools for understanding and communicating sea-level rise impacts.

Data Visualization

Interactive maps and charts that bring sea-level rise projections to life with intuitive, scientifically accurate visualizations.

Risk Assessment

Quantify coastal vulnerability with multi-scenario modeling based on IPCC projections and regional sea-level data.

Scenario Planning

Compare different emission pathways and adaptation strategies to support evidence-based decision making.

Export & Share

Generate publication-ready graphics and reports, or export raw data for further analysis in your workflow.

Open Source

Completely transparent methodology and code. Fork, modify, and contribute to advance coastal climate science.

Community Driven

Built by and for the coastal science community. Your feedback shapes the future of this tool.

Addressing a Global Challenge

Sea-level rise threatens coastal communities, infrastructure, and ecosystems worldwide. By 2100, hundreds of millions of people could be affected by coastal flooding and erosion.

OSLRAT democratizes access to sea-level rise data and projections, enabling communities of all sizes to understand their risks and plan for a changing climate.
